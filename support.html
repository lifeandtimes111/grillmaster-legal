Frequently asked

Do I have to keep the app open while grilling?
No. GrillMaster sends a notification for every cue, flip, pull, and so on, even when your phone is locked or in your pocket.

What's free and what's Pro?
Every food is free, including steak and steak tips, with the full timeline, alerts, and Apple Watch cues. GrillMaster Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks spoken voice cues, weather-smart timing, custom preheat, and doneness and thickness controls.

How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?
Open the Pro screen and tap "Restore Purchase."

The times seem off for my grill.
Grills vary. GrillMaster Pro lets you set your real preheat temperature and each cut's thickness and doneness for a closer estimate. Always confirm doneness with a meat thermometer.
