GrillMaster Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

GrillMaster ("the app") is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not collect.

Information we collect

None. GrillMaster does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There is no account, no login, and no analytics or tracking.

ZIP code

If you enter a ZIP code for weather-smart timing, it is used only on your device to estimate cook times and is never sent to us or any third party.

Purchases

The one-time GrillMaster Pro unlock is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment information. Purchase status is managed by Apple's StoreKit on your device.

Notifications

GrillMaster schedules local notifications on your device to alert you to grilling cues. These are generated and delivered locally by iOS and are not sent through any server.

Children

GrillMaster is rated 4+ and does not collect data from anyone, including children.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
